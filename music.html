Music

Music—classical piano—is my longest-running hobby.

Hobby, in that it has never sustained me financially, and in that it is not what I studied as an undergraduate. The word feels unfair. But what words are there for that which you begin at age four and continue for the rest of your life?

I could say music is my life, but that feels cliche at best, and unimaginably reductive at worst. No, my life has meaning far beyond my music. I could say music is my joy, but in fact it is music that frustrates and angers me most.
